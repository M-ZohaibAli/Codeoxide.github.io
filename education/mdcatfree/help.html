Formula

  • some important formulas for each chapter of federal class 11 physics:


    Chapter 1: Measurements

    - Accuracy = [(|True Value - Measured Value|)/True Value] * 100%
    - Precision = (Standard Deviation / Mean Value) * 100%

    Chapter 2: Vectors and Equilibrium
    - Resultant Force (R) = √((R_x)² + (R_y)²)
    - Resolving Force (F_x) = F cosθ
    - Resolving Force (F_y) = F sinθ

    Chapter 3: Motion and Force
    - Acceleration (a) = (v_f - v_i) / t
    - Force (F) = m * a
    - Kinetic Energy (K) = (1/2) * m * v²

    Chapter 4: Work and Energy
    - Work (W) = F * d * cosθ
    - Potential Energy (U) = m * g * h
    - Mechanical Energy (E) = K + U


    Chapter 5: Circular Motion
    - Centripetal Force (F_c) = (mv²) / r
    - Centripetal Acceleration (a_c) = (v²) / r
    - Angular Velocity (ω) = θ / t


    Chapter 6: Fluid Dynamics
    - Pressure (P) = F / A
    - Density (ρ) = m / V
    - Buoyant Force (F_b) = ρ * V * g


    Chapter 7: Oscillations
    - Simple Harmonic Motion: x = Asin(ωt + φ)
    - Period (T) = (2π) / ω
    - Frequency (f) = 1 / T


    Chapter 8: Waves
    - Wave Velocity (v) = fλ
    - Wavelength (λ) = v/f
    - Frequency (f) = v / λ


    Chapter 9: Physical Optics
    - Snell’s Law: n_1 sinθ_1 = n_2 sinθ_2
    - Lens Formula: (1/f) = (1/v) + (1/u)
    - Magnification (m) = -v/u
