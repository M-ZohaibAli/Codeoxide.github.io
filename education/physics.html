Physics

Physics is one of the most fundamental scientific disciplines, the main goal of which is to understand how the universe behaves. Here you will love the ways physics concepts are explained.




Nuclear physics

Nuclear physics is the study of the behavior of atomic nuclei and the particles that make them up. This includes topics such as nuclear reactions, radioactive decay etc.



Mechanics

Mechanics is the study of the motion of objects and the forces that cause them to move. This includes topics such as kinematics, dynamics, and energy.



Astrophysics

Astrophysics is the study of the behavior of objects in the universe, including stars, galaxies, and the larger structure of the universe.



Relativity

Relativity is the study of the behavior of objects at high speeds and in strong gravitational fields. This includes topics such as special relativity, general relativity, and black holes.



Quantum Physics

Quantum mechanics is the study of the behavior of particles on the atomic and subatomic level. This includes topics such as wave-particle duality, the uncertainty principle, and quantum entanglement.



Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics is the study of heat and its relationship to other forms of energy. This includes topics such as temperature, heat transfer, and the laws of thermodynamics.
